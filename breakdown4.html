Element

A substance that cannot chemically be broken down into simpler ingredients. Made of atoms with the same atomic number (number of protons).

Stir Together

More elements can be mixed together by forming chemical bonds.

Compound

A combination of two or more different elements. This is the form you will most often encounter. Few elements are found uncombined in nature.
